Welcome to Chicago

Chicago, the third-largest city in the United States, is known for its stunning architecture, vibrant arts scene, and diverse culinary experiences.

Fact Details
Population Approximately 2.7 million
Year Incorporated 1837
Region Northeastern Illinois
Classification City
Average Income Varies by district; Approximately $70,000 per year

Discover the rich history and culture of Chicago by visiting its museums, parks, and neighborhoods.

Millennium Park

Millennium Park is a public park located in the Loop area of Chicago, famous for its innovative art, architecture, and landscape design, including the Cloud Gate sculpture.
