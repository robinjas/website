Welcome to Springfield

Springfield, the capital city of Illinois, is steeped in the history of Abraham Lincoln, with numerous historical sites dedicated to the 16th President of the United States.

Fact Details
Population Approximately 114,000
Year Incorporated 1832
Region Central Illinois
Classification Urban
Average Income Close to state average ($60,000 per year)

Explore the Abraham Lincoln Presidential Library and Museum, visit the historic Old State Capitol, and walk in the footsteps of one of America's greatest leaders.

Lincoln Home National Historic Site

The Lincoln Home National Historic Site offers a glimpse into the life of Abraham Lincoln with guided tours of his beautiful family home, preserved in its 1860s condition.
