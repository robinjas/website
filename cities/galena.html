Welcome to Galena

Nestled in the northwest corner of Illinois, Galena is a living museum of 19th-century architecture, with over 85% of its buildings listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

Fact Details
Population Approximately 3,200
Year Incorporated 1826
Region Northwestern Illinois
Classification City
Average Income Approximately $48,000 per year

Visitors can explore its historic Main Street, enjoy scenic views of the Galena River, and partake in the numerous festivals and events that celebrate Galena's rich history and vibrant community.

Ulysses S. Grant Home

The Ulysses S. Grant Home is a key attraction in Galena, offering insight into the life of the 18th President of the United States and Civil War general, with the home preserved much as it was in the 1860s.
