Mission Statement

Discover Illinois is your premier online resource for information about the great state of Illinois. As your gateway to state governance, we aim to provide comprehensive and accurate details about the cities, culture, and unique features that make Illinois exceptional. From the bustling streets of Chicago to the historic charm of Galena, discover what makes Illinois a fascinating place to explore.

Fun fact!

Did you know that Illinois is known as the "Land of Lincoln" because it was the home of President Abraham Lincoln before he became president? Lincoln moved to Illinois in 1830 and later practiced law in Springfield. The state's rich history includes its pivotal role in shaping American politics and society.
